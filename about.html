Being immersed in the world of Polynesian dance since youth, my sense of appreciation for art was never bound. It was through cultural dance that I not only developed my love for creative exploration but also my appreciation for culture and design.

As a child I built Xanga layouts and loved every minute of it. After studying journalism and marketing I transitioned back to web design as a more powerful outlet to express my creativity and passion for visual asthetics. I am a designer, developer and overall problem solver seeking beauty in everything.

My time spent away from the screen is often filled with nature and yoga, the basic simplicities such as this are the inspiration for my minimal design style. I believe if the essentials are beautiful and properly done, there is no need for more. I have dipped my toe in various elements of design from music composition to clothing creation.

At this moment in time I am a responsive front end designer focusing on efficiecy and minimalism to develop effective user-centric solutions When I'm not coding you can find me wandering through the frighteningly tall redwoods, planning my next swimming hole adventure or looking for new ways to explore creativity.
