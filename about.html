Web Designer, Developer and Marketer

I help local entrepreneurs and nonprofits stand out with the perfect website. I’ll create a brand strategy, a website design and code it for you. If you want, I can also help you write the copy - all the headlines, descriptions and general text on your site!

I use bold, attention-grabbing colors paired with effortless designs that lead customers right into your arms! I take a minimalist approach to web design because I want customers to focus on what you have to offer! A great website should be save time, keep it real and get right to the point. I enjoy working with people who are self-motivated, perceptive and flexible with their creative process.

I spend my nights coding and my days marketing for Sports Basement. After some experience managing Wordpress sites for HUSHconcerts, I realized I wanted to combine my love of design with my marketing know-how. I was already familiar with HTML and CSS from designing Xanga layouts in middle school, but I polished my tech skills in a web design and development program at Skillcrush.

When I’m not working, I like dancing, camping, hula hooping, Giants baseball, cooking, stretching, designing clothes and starting new art projects if there’s still time. I’m a color fiend and a print fanatic. You can pretty much guarantee I’ll be wearing printed leggings or a graphic tee any day of the week.
